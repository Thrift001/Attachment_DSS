Project Background

The Somalia Renewable Energy Decision Support System (DSS) is a strategic initiative by ADRA Somalia designed to leverage geospatial intelligence for sustainable development. By integrating Global Horizontal Irradiance (GHI) and Wind Power Density (WPD) datasets, this system identifies high-potential zones for decentralized energy infrastructure.

Our objective is to streamline the implementation of solar-powered water pumping and community microgrids across the federal states. This tool enables field officers and planners to make evidence-based decisions, reducing engineering risks associated with terrain topography and optimizing resource allocation for climate-vulnerable communities.
